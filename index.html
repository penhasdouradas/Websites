PENHAS DOURADAS

Soundcloud — Bandcamp — Instagram — Youtube

Penhas Douradas is the music project of Portuguese-born João Chaves. Inspired by the serene landscapes and quiet introspection of the mountains, and named after a region close to where the artist grew up, the name carries a deeply personal connection, a place that often stood in for home on weather forecasts, becoming a subtle but meaningful symbol of belonging.

Rooted in memories of misty hills, changing seasons, and the ever-present stillness of nature, Penhas Douradas explores sound as a gentle invitation to slow down. Through slow-moving textures, delicate sonic explorations, and meditative soundscapes, it opens spaces for reflection, inner listening, and grounding — a way to reconnect with both the earth and oneself.

"Sabedoria Interior" is set to be released on 16th of May in all major streaming platforms
